Electronic Devices

Electronic devices are tools or systems that operate using the flow of electrons through circuits to perform specific functions such as communication, computation, control, or entertainment. Common examples include smartphones, computers, televisions, and digital watches. These devices rely on various components like resistors, capacitors, transistors, and microchips to process electrical signals. With advancements in technology, electronic devices have become more compact, efficient, and powerful, playing a crucial role in modern life by improving convenience, connectivity, and productivity across all sectors.
